Proprietor’s Address

Congratulations! You have just made the right decision by choosing Ebunoluwa International School (EIS) as your school. This is a citadel of academic excellence located in a beautiful and serene environment.

This is a school where students are led to discover themselves and have an all-round development. You are also guided, trained and directed towards becoming reputable, diligent, patriotic and responsible citizens of this great country – Nigeria.

A new breed of Nigerians is being raised here under a pleasant, friendly, accommodating and conducive atmosphere.

Always bear in mind the primary objective of your coming here, which is to attain sound education and to predicate that sound education on good morals.
